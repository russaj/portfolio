Writing

Ever since I can remember, I have loved to write. From writing fan fiction to writing in my high school newspaper, I always wanted to craft stories to entertain and inspire others. Below are my published works, a list I hope to continue to grow.



"A Hoosier Abroad"

Written for a crowd-funded book titled Undeniably Indiana which celebrated Indiana’s Bicentennial, this short story reflects on the time I was in a Greek cab in Athens, reflecting about my home state after the cab driver asked where I was from and what it was known for.



MBA Insider

I have been featured in a book, MBA Insider, How to Make the Most of Your MBA Experience by Al Dea. This book is a fantastic guide to understanding everything you need to know about business school. It’s a great book for prospective and current MBA students who want to learn best practices and expert tips for navigating business school, and has student stories from over 50 MBA students and alum.
